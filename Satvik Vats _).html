A little about me

Woo! I am a Sophomore at University Of Washington, Seattle. Five years down the line I see myself as an Innovator, entrepreneur and a popularizer of Science. I love listening to music and playing some badmintton (I am pretty good at it). I love to do team research, I have been part of many researches, out of which one of them got published in an International Journal. I am always in for intellectual discussions and would love to connect with people having similar or diverse interests. This site showcases some of my work.
I got 99 Problems but design 'aint one

I try to do amazing things

Video making

Video Prototyping can be a very powerful tool to communicate a design. I have had multiple opportunities where I had to make a design video prototype, and have always helped me refine my design to my audience. Great skill to have.

UX Designer

Uff I just love what I do! Finding design solutions to problems using user research, testing and brainstroming thrills me! I am always excited to learn, from solving Wikipedia censorship to navigation for blinds using holophonic sounds, never felt better!

Design Integration

Design is a great tool to impact people. How use of A.I., deep learning and 3D printing with design can make earth a better place to live, facinates me! I want to learn more about these and shorten the list of problems I want to solve before dying.
